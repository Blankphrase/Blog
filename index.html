How to prepare Mandazis

Mandazi is a delicacy and snack that is slowly spreading all over the continent. Its preparation is easy and requirres afew additions to get the requirred output

Ingredients needed

  • Self raising flour
  • Baking powder
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Milk
  • Margarine
  • Cinnamon Flavour
  • Eggs

Assemblance

  • Boil water till luke warm temperature
  • Measure the Margarine
  • Measure the sugar

Preparation

  • In a tin, beat the margarine and the sugar until light and fluffy
  • Beat in the eggs one at a time until well incorporated
  • Mix in the flour and the baking powder and mix well
  • Add in milk and knead the dough if not well incorporated
  • Add in water
  • Add the cinnamon and knead dough till you get the desired texture
